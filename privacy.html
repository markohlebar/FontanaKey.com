Privacy Policy

Keystrokes

  • Fontana is not tracking your keystrokes. It is using whatever information is in the current typing context that is marked between :: to make a request to the popular search engines to get your search results.

    • History

  • Fontana is not uploading your history. Your usage of Fontana is stored within the app, and it is erased when you erase the app.

    • Tracking

  • Fontana is tracking the usage of the app and keyboard to a reasonable level by using third party tracking solutions, so we can make improvements if necessary. We are however not tracking your search queries.
